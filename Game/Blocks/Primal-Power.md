### Primal Power

_You have learned to tap into your primal force to lend you aid._

- You may spend a [Power](Game/Core/Blocks/Power) to perform some action that would seem impossible for an average human.

> Hold your breath for 30 minutes, lift a vehicle, run immeasurably fast.
